About Us

Our story began in 2005. We are classified as non-government company and are registered at Registrar of Companies, Goa. Today, we are privileged to connect with thousands of people and are involved in Restaurants, bars and canteens.

Villa Blanche is the name which should strike your mind when in doubt what to eat. No matter what food cuisine being Italian and continental as its speciality, or a mix of international and Goan food or a perfect Sunday brunch, also if you are looking for the best desserts and cookies in town. Along with the food, this is a great place to drop by if you are looking to sit in a calm atmosphere, meet travellers, or a cosy and a light ambience with bamboo chairs.
